Featured Title

Tales from the Towpath

Gene Earl Thompson

In this collection drawn from his journals and short stories, Thompson reflects on life, love, loss, mothers, and finding more than a bit of humor in daily life. Assembled posthumously by his grandchildren, the book is the result of many difficult choices guided by a long engagement with the author and his writing.

Submissions

Towpath Publishing's mentorship program is currently open to a small number of submissions. We are interested in American stories, including memoir, short fiction, essays, and regional writing. This program is intended for authors pursuing self-publishing who are looking for guidance, feedback, and perspective—not traditional publication.

Please include a brief pitch, a short bio, and either the first ten pages or up to three short pieces.
